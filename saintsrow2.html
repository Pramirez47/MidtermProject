About

Saints Row 2 is an action-adventure game set in the open world of Stilwater, a city with depth and a story to tell. Following the success of the first game, Saints Row 2 features a more expansive narrative and a world twice the size of its predecessor. Players can explore the city, engage in various activities, and rise through the ranks of the Saints, a notorious gang battling for control and respect.
